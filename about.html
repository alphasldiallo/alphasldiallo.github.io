Executive summary

I am an IT professional with more than ten years of experience in consulting, software engineering and project management. I recently completed a PhD in Information Systems at the Distributed Object Programming Lab of HEC Lausanne (University of Lausanne — Switzerland). My area of research was related to Indoor Positioning, Human Mobility, and Distributed Systems.

During my master's in Information Systems, I worked on indoor tracking systems by implementing a system that balanced ubiquity, accuracy, and cost. Prior to this experience, I led the development of the national portal of Investment for the Republic of Guinea and, along with a team, made a mapping of the Guinean private sector as a consultant for a World Bank Project (IDA H 8670 – GN).

My area of expertise ranges from collecting, analyzing and modeling location data as well as managing IT projects.
