I recently completed a PhD in Information Systems at the [Distributed Object Programming Lab](http://doplab.unil.ch) of HEC Lausanne (University of Lausanne - Switzerland). My area of research was related to Indoor Positioning, Human Mobility, and Distributed Systems. Prior to this experience, I led the development of a national portal of Investment for the Republic of Guinea and, along with a team, made a mapping of the Guinean private sector as a consultant for a World Bank Project (IDA H 8670 – GN).
